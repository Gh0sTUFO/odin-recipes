Spaghetti aglio e olio Recipe:

Description:>

This classic Italian dish is as simple as it gets, but don't be fooled by its minimalist nature! Thinly sliced garlic and a kick of red pepper flakes dance together in a pan with fragrant olive oil, creating a tantalizing aroma that will have your taste buds doing the mambo. Toss this flavorful sauce with al dente spaghetti, sprinkle with fresh parsley, and voila! You've got a plate of pasta that will make you say "Holy cannoli, that's delicious!"

Ingredients:

  • 8 ounces (225 grams) spaghetti
  • 4 cloves garlic, thinly sliced
  • 1/4 cup extra virgin olive oil
  • 1/2 teaspoon red pepper flakes (adjust according to your spice preference)
  • Salt, to taste
  • Freshly ground black pepper, to taste
  • 1/4 cup chopped fresh parsley
  • Grated Parmesan cheese (optional)

Steps:

  1. Cook the spaghetti in a large pot of salted boiling water according to the package instructions until al dente.
  2. While the pasta is cooking, heat the olive oil in a large skillet over medium heat. Add the garlic slices and red pepper flakes to the skillet.
  3. Cook the garlic and pepper flakes, stirring frequently, until the garlic turns golden brown and fragrant. Be careful not to burn the garlic.
  4. Remove the skillet from heat and set it aside.
  5. Once the spaghetti is cooked, drain it, reserving about 1/2 cup of the pasta cooking water.
  6. Add the drained spaghetti to the skillet with the garlic and oil mixture.
  7. Toss the spaghetti with the garlic and oil, coating it evenly. If the pasta seems dry, add a splash of the reserved pasta cooking water to moisten it.
  8. Season the spaghetti with salt and freshly ground black pepper to taste. Toss again to combine.
  9. Stir in the chopped parsley.
  10. Serve the spaghetti aglio e olio immediately, garnished with grated Parmesan cheese if desired.
