Guacamole Recipe:

Description:

Get ready to "avo" great time with this easy homemade guacamole recipe! Creamy avocados are mashed to perfection and mixed with zesty lime juice, tangy red onions, juicy tomatoes, and a hint of garlic. It's a fiesta in your mouth! Whip up this crowd-pleasing dip and watch it disappear faster than you can say "guac and roll"!

Ingredients:

  • 2 ripe avocados
  • 1 lime
  • 1/4 cup diced red onion
  • 1 small tomato, diced
  • 2 tablespoons chopped fresh cilantro
  • 1 clove garlic, minced
  • Salt and pepper to taste

Steps:

  1. Cut the avocados in half and remove the pits. Scoop out the flesh into a bowl.
  2. Squeeze the juice of the lime over the avocado and mash it with a fork or potato masher until it reaches your desired consistency.
  3. Add the diced red onion, tomato, cilantro, and minced garlic to the bowl.
  4. Season with salt and pepper to taste.
  5. Mix everything together until well combined.
  6. Taste and adjust the seasoning if needed.
  7. Serve immediately with tortilla chips or as a topping for tacos, nachos, or sandwiches.
