Ingredients:

  • 4 boneless, skinless chicken breasts
  • 2 tablespoons olive oil
  • 1 teaspoon garlic powder
  • 1 teaspoon paprika
  • 1/2 teaspoon salt
  • 1/4 teaspoon black pepper
  • Optional: additional herbs and spices of your choice (e.g., dried thyme, oregano, rosemary)

Steps:

  1. Preheat your oven to 400째F (200째C). Lightly grease a baking dish or line it with parchment paper.
  2. Place the chicken breasts on the baking dish.
  3. Drizzle the olive oil over the chicken breasts, then rub them to coat evenly.
  4. In a small bowl, mix together the garlic powder, paprika, salt, black pepper, and any additional herbs or spices you'd like to use.
  5. Sprinkle the seasoning mixture over the chicken breasts, coating both sides.
  6. Place the baking dish with the seasoned chicken breasts in the preheated oven.
  7. Bake for about 20-25 minutes or until the chicken is cooked through and reaches an internal temperature of 165째F (74째C).
  8. Once cooked, remove the chicken breasts from the oven and let them rest for a few minutes before serving.
  9. Slice or serve the chicken breasts whole, as desired.
